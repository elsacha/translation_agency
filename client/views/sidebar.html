Become a translator

Translation and interpreting (oral translation) is a very demanding skill. If you want to become a translator, you have to master not only a few foreign languages, but also the culture and civilization of the countries where these languages are spoken.

When you feel you are ready to study translation/interpreting, we suggest you enroll in a translation school which provides specialized training.

Machine translation

Nowadays, computers are everywhere. They manage the most complex industrial processes, send sattelites to Mars or sequence the human genome. Will they replace translators too?

Although such systems as Google Translate can provide a decent translation, this translation will be far from perfect and will need editing. Almighty computers have not yet learned to think as a human translator or a human in general. It may happen one day, but until then we are happy to provide you our services.
